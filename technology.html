Typing Speed and Smartphones in the Classroom:

Typing Speed:
When I took my typing speed test, my average typing speed was 74 words per minute.

Smartphones in the Classroom:

I believe, if used for educational purposes, samrtphones could have a place in the classroom. The only issue with it would be that there would be no point of using a smartphone when you could have a laptop instead. They are more practical and could be less tempting to play games on when in the classroom. I believe that smartphones should not even be considered as a educational device as there is to much else to do on them you could be drawn in by instead of doing the required task at hand.
